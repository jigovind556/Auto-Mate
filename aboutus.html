We, the sophomores of NIT Kurukshetra have build this Web-App, solving a major travel-issue of students.This software solves the problem of solo travelers of NIT Kurukshetra who ought to hire a full auto for a ride that demands a special fare that is expensive. It brings together all who want to travel in the same time period, allowing them to share the auto, thus minimizing the auto fare for each person.
